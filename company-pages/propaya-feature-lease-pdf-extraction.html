Frequently asked questions

Who should use Propaya?

At Propaya, we are dedicated to supporting everyone in need of property management solutions. While our primary clients are property managers overseeing multiple properties, we warmly welcome anyone requiring assistance with managing leases. Whether you're a property manager or someone with several leases to handle, our automated processes are designed to simplify your workload and make property management more efficient. Propaya is here to help, no matter your specific needs.

What is required to use Propaya?

To use Propaya, you will primarily need property leases. Our platform is designed to automate and streamline property management by transferring data from PDF leases into autopopulated sections. This automation ensures efficiency and accuracy, making it easier for you to manage your leases effortlessly. Whether you have a few leases or manage multiple properties, having your lease documents ready is the key to unlocking the full potential of Propaya.

Why is Propaya different?

Many property managers and property management applications on the market today handle property management tasks manually, requiring frequent updates and changes. Propaya sets itself apart by offering an automated and hassle-free solution. With just a click of a button, our platform can autopopulate numerous data points, including security deposit details and ledger information. This automation significantly reduces the time and effort required for property management, providing a seamless experience that our competitors cannot match.
