Lease Extraction

Using cutting edge technology, Propaya is able to extract important information from uploaded or scanned leases.

  • Accuracy: Our extractor works with exceptional accuracy, ensuring you have reliable data to work with.
  • Rapid Processing: Leases are analyzed in mere seconds, saving you valuable time and effort.
  • Comprehensive Data Capture: Propaya extracts essential details such as HOA information, security deposit amounts, rent terms, and more, providing a complete overview of lease agreements.

Automated Calendar

Keep track of all of your upcoming events with Propaya's automated calendar. Any information in your database will be automatically added to your calendar, if applicable.

  • Automated Reminders: Automatically set reminders for important deadlines, such as rent payments, lease renewals, and maintenance appointments.
  • Personalized View: Customize your calendar view to suit your preferences, whether you prefer a daily, weekly, or monthly overview.
  • Real-Time Updates: Propaya's calendar automatically updates to reflect changes in your schedule, ensuring you always have the most accurate information.
